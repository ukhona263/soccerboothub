Privacy Policy

At BootHub, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Learn how we handle your personal information.

Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines how BootHub collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, purchase our products, or use our services. We are dedicated to ensuring transparency and safeguarding your data in accordance with applicable laws, including GDPR and CCPA.

Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address, and payment details provided during orders or account creation.
  • Usage Data: Browsing history, IP address, device type, and interactions with our website (e.g., clicks, page views).
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to enhance site functionality, personalize content, and analyze traffic.
  • Service Data: Information from fitting appointments or performance analysis, such as foot measurements or playing style preferences.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Process and fulfill orders, including shipping and returns.
  • Provide personalized services, such as custom boot fittings.
  • Send promotional emails or newsletters (with your consent).
  • Improve our website, products, and customer experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party partners for payment processing, shipping, and analytics (e.g., Stripe, Google Analytics).
  • Brand Partners: Companies like Nike or Adidas for co-branded promotions, with your consent.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption for payment transactions and sensitive data.
  • Secure servers and regular security audits.
  • Access controls to limit data exposure.

While we strive to protect your information, no system is completely secure. Please safeguard your account credentials.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability or restrict processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@boothub.com.
